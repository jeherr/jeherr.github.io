I'm a research scientist currently at the University of Notre Dame. I just finished my PhD in the Spring 2020
semester, having spent three years with Dr. John Parkhill as an adviser and two years with Drs. Olaf Wiest and Paul
Helquist as co-advisers. I received my Associate of Arts from Southeastern Community College in Iowa, and bachelor's
degrees in Chemistry and Mathematics from the University of Iowa. Upon receiving my PhD in May of 2020 I will be
continuing to work with Dr. Wiest as a part of the Center for Computer Assisted Synthesis through August before
to a Postdoctoral research position.

Research Interests
======
My research lies at the interface of chemistry and machine learning. Recently, neural networks have
seen renewed interest in the computer science field and elsewhere. In chemistry there has been an influx
of research using neural network models for a wide array of applications.
